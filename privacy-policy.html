THE CAR TETOUAN MOBILITY

Privacy Policy

THE CAR Tetouan Mobility is designed to provide a safe and simple way to view rental vehicles and contact our team through WhatsApp or email. The app does not include online payment and does not collect personal or sensitive data automatically.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, process, share, or sell personal data inside the app. The app is used to display vehicle details and pricing.

2. Permissions

The app does not request access to location, contacts, camera, microphone, storage, or other sensitive device data.

3. Images and Content

Vehicle images and details are provided for informational purposes only.

4. How the App Works

Users can browse available vehicles, review pricing, and contact the team using WhatsApp or email.

5. Price Calculator

The calculator feature runs locally on your device to estimate rental totals based on values you enter. No calculator input is sent or stored by the app.

6. Security

The app minimizes data handling and does not keep personal calculation data in local storage.

7. Third-Party Services

The app does not include analytics, ads, or tracking SDKs. Any communication through WhatsApp or email follows their own policies.

8. Child Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13 years old.
